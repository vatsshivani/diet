Good To Know

Why Should I Spend Time With A Nutritionist ?

A Nutritionist can individualize your nutrition counselling experience, taking into account your medical and family history, your lifestyle and preferences and help you achieve realistic goals to improve your health.

What results will I see from my session ?

Depending on the goals you set with your Nutritionist, you can increase energy levels, lose weight, build muscle mass, optimize your nutrition in pregnancy, lower cholesterol levels, prevent or manage chronic diseases like diabetes and osteoporosis, just to name a few

What is the difference between Nutritionist & other nutritian or weight loss program ?

A Nutritionist is the only nutrition or weight-loss specialist to provide safe and effective nutrition counselling services. A Nutritionist's knowledge is based on a university education, current evidence-based research and clinical experience.

How often do I need to see a Nutritionist ?

To start with, you need to come for a 1 hour initial assessment. At this session, the Nutritionist gathers information such as your medical, social and diet history. Depending on the goals you set with your Nutritionist, you may want to come back for follow up sessions to receive a detailed meal plan, diet analysis or for accountability and adjustment of your nutrition goals.
